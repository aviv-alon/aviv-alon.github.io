About Me

Since I was a child I was fascinated by technology. At the age of four, I took apart my first computer. At the age of 11, I could put it back together and wrote my first program. Since then, I graduated with a degree in information technology with honours, served as the Linux System Administrator for the Israeli Air Force Computing Unit and worked at the largest ISP company in Israel as a System Engineer.

After 5 years in Bezeq international, I have been studying M.sc degree in computer science at Ben Gurion University and have taken time out to complete a course in Web Development at General Assembly in London.
